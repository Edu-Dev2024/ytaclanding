About the Event

Aimed at students and young enthusiasts, the hackathon provides a platform for participants to tackle real-world problems through coding, collaboration, and creativity. By combining teamwork with individual skill development, the hackathon encourages participants to think critically and develop practical solutions to everyday technical challenges.
